From the moment you're served to the moment your case is resolved, here's exactly how The Fullman Firm defends you.

Step 1
You Get Served
You receive a lawsuit, summons, or complaint from a creditor or debt collector.
Don't panic — and don't ignore it. When you're served with a debt lawsuit in California, you typically have 30 days to respond. If you fail to respond, the court will enter a default judgment against you, allowing the creditor to garnish your wages, levy your bank account, or put a lien on your property. Time is critical.
